Feature Graphic Template Instructions

This is a template for your Google Play Store feature graphic. Please follow these steps:

  1. Take a screenshot of this graphic (1024 x 500 pixels)
  2. Optionally customize in a design tool like Canva, Photoshop, or GIMP
  3. Save as PNG or JPEG format
  4. Place in your store-assets folder as "feature_graphic.png"

Design Tips:

  • Keep text minimal - focus on your brand name and a short tagline
  • Use your app's color scheme consistently
  • Make sure text is readable at smaller sizes
  • Consider replacing emoji icons with actual furniture product images
  • Test how it looks on different devices
